Minecraft

Minecraft is a 3D sandbox game developed by Mojang Studios that offers players an open-ended experience in a procedurally generated world made of blocks. Players can gather resources, craft tools, build structures, and explore diverse biomes and dimensions.

Roblox

Roblox is an online platform and game creation system developed by Roblox Corporation, allowing users to create, share, and play games made by the community. Released in 2006, it features millions of user-generated "experiences" across genres like role-playing, adventure, simulation, and action. Games are created using Roblox Studio, which employs the Lua programming language

Fortnite

Fortnite, developed by Epic Games, is a popular online video game featuring multiple modes. The most well-known is Fortnite Battle Royale, where up to 100 players compete to be the last one standing on a shrinking map. Players skydive onto an island, gather weapons and resources, and use unique building mechanics to construct fortifications for defense or strategic advantages. The game also includes Zero Build Mode, which removes construction elements for a more traditional battle royale experience
